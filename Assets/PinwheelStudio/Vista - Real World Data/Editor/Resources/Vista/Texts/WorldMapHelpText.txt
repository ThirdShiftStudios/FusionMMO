NAVIGATION
Use the left mouse to pan.
Use the scrollwheel to zoom.
Press "F" to focus on the selected region.

SELECTING A REGION
Click on the 'Select a region' button.
Drag on the map to draw a rectangle.
Can draw again if you want.
Click on the 'Done' button to go back to navigation mode.
